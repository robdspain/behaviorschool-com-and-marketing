## Troubleshooting

### Newsletter Not Sending
- Check Listmonk SMTP settings
- Verify subscriber list is not empty
- Test with single email address first
- Check spam folder

### Low Open Rates (<20%)
- Test different subject lines (A/B test)
- Send at different times (test 9am vs 6pm)
- Segment audience (exam prep vs school BCBAs)
- Clean inactive subscribers (>6 months no open)

### Low Click Rates (<3%)
- Make CTAs more prominent
- Reduce number of links (focus on 1 primary action)
- Test different button text
- Add urgency/scarcity ("Limited time")

### Social Posts Not Getting Engagement
- Post at different times (test)
- Use more visuals (images, diagrams)
- Ask questions to prompt comments
- Tag relevant accounts
- Use 1-2 fewer hashtags
