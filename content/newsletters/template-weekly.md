# Weekly Newsletter Template for School-Based BCBAs

## Newsletter Structure

### 1. Welcome / Opening
**Tone:** Friendly, professional, empathetic
**Length:** 2-3 sentences

Example:
> Hey [First Name],
> 
> Hope your week is going well! I know how overwhelming it can get juggling FBAs, IEPs, and everything else. This week, I've got some practical tips that can save you time and reduce stress.

---

### 2. Featured Tip / Strategy (Main Content)
**Format:** One actionable, evidence-based strategy
**Length:** 150-200 words

**Template:**
```
## [Catchy Title with Benefit]

**The Problem:** [Describe a common pain point school BCBAs face]

**The Solution:** [Your evidence-based strategy]

**How to Use It:**
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

**Why It Works:** [Brief explanation with research backing]

**Try This:** [Quick action item they can implement this week]
```

**Example Topics:**
- 5-Minute FBA Framework for Busy BCBAs
- Getting Teacher Buy-In Without the Pushback
- Data Collection That Actually Gets Used
- Crisis Prevention Strategies That Work
- Making IEP Goals Measurable (and Meaningful)
- Time Management for School BCBAs
- Building Rapport with Resistant Staff
- When to Say "No" to Referrals

---

### 3. Quick Win / Tool of the Week
**Format:** One small, immediately actionable tip or free resource
**Length:** 50-75 words

**Template:**
```
## Quick Win: [Title]

[1-2 sentence description of the tool/tip]

**Get it:** [Link or quick how-to]
```

**Examples:**
- Free ABC Data Sheet Template
- Google Form for Teacher Input
- Visual Schedule Template
- Behavior Contract Template
- Parent Communication Email Templates
- Classroom Observation Checklist

---

### 4. Research Highlight (Optional)
**Format:** Recent study explained in plain language
**Length:** 75-100 words

**Template:**
```
## Research You Can Use

**Study:** [Brief title and citation]

**What They Found:** [Key finding in 1-2 sentences]

**Why It Matters:** [Practical application for school BCBAs]

[Link to full study or summary]
```

---

### 5. Community Spotlight (Optional)
**Format:** Success story, reader question, or member highlight
**Length:** 50-100 words

Example:
> **Reader Win:** Sarah (BCBA in Oregon) shared how she used last week's strategy to cut her FBA report time in half. She's now leaving school by 4 PM most days!

---

### 6. Closing / CTA
**Tone:** Warm, supportive, encouraging
**Include:** One clear call-to-action

**Template:**
```
---

**What's on your mind this week?** Hit reply and let me know what challenges you're facing. I read every email and often turn reader questions into future tips!

**Want more?**
- [Link to latest blog post]
- [Free resource download]
- [Upcoming webinar/event]

Take care,  
Rob Spain, M.S., BCBA, IBA  
[Behavior School](https://behaviorschool.com)

P.S. [Optional: Brief personal note, upcoming content teaser, or timely tip]
```

---

## Newsletter Best Practices

### Timing
- **Send:** Every Tuesday or Wednesday (mid-week, when BCBAs are busiest and need support)
- **Time:** 6:00 AM PST (catches morning email check)

### Length
- **Total:** 300-500 words (2-3 minute read)
- **Frequency:** Weekly (consistent is better than perfect)

### Subject Lines
**Formula:** [Benefit] + [Time Frame/Ease]

**Examples:**
- "Cut your FBA time in half (here's how)"
- "3 ways to get teachers on board this week"
- "The 5-minute strategy that stops classroom disruptions"
- "What to do when admin pushes back (script included)"
- "Data collection that actually works"

### Voice/Tone
- Conversational but professional
- Evidence-based but practical
- Empathetic to struggles
- Solution-focused
- Brief and scannable

## Email List Segments (Future)

Once your list grows, segment by:
- **New BCBAs** (0-2 years) - More foundational content
- **Experienced BCBAs** (3+ years) - Advanced strategies
- **District Leaders** - Systems-level content
- **Private Practice** - Different pain points

---

## Metrics to Track

- Open rate (target: >25%)
- Click-through rate (target: >3%)
- Reply rate (engagement indicator)
- Unsubscribe rate (keep <0.5%)
- Conversion to paid programs

**Ready to Start?**

Create your first newsletter by:
1. Pick one strategy from your recent blog posts
2. Follow the template above
3. Keep it under 400 words
4. Send every Tuesday at 6 AM
5. Ask one question to encourage replies

Start simple, stay consistent, and adjust based on what your subscribers engage with most!
